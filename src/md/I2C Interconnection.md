
[Communication between Raspberry Pi and Arduino with I2C](https://www.aranacorp.com/en/communication-between-raspberry-pi-and-arduino-with-i2c/)

    SDA BCM2(RPI) <-> SDA A4(Arduino)
    SCL BCM3(RPI) <-> SCL A5(Arduino)
    GND (RPI) <-> GND(Arduino)
